Job Description

We are looking for a web frontend engineer to join PayPay. You will be building features that change how people in Japan use and manage their finances and business. As a fast-paced company leading the payments industry, we need our engineers to be versatile and enthusiastic to take on new challenges in order to stay at the forefront.

 

Responsibilities

  • Work with Product Managers, Designers and various tech teams to implement features.
  • Contribute to the team by sharing knowledge, participating in code reviews, and providing feedback to uphold and ensure best practices.
  • Triage, debug, and resolve product issues.

 

Tech Stack

｜TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML/CSS
｜Vue.js, React, NestJS, Express.js, single-spa
｜Docker, Kubernetes, ArgoCD, AWS, GH Actions, Terraform, Gatling
｜GitHub, npm/yarn, Jenkins, Webpack, Jest, i18n, Storybook
｜Sentry, Kibana, Google Analytics, Firebase, NewRelic
｜Figma, PlantUML, draw.io
｜Slack, Zoom, Confluence, Jira

 

Required Qualifications

  • At least 4 years of industry experience developing secure web frontend applications in JavaScript and/or other transpile-to-JavaScript languages.
  • At least 1 year of industry experience with VueJS (preferred), or React.
  • Software engineering best practices and computer science knowledge.
  • Business level of English.

 

Additional Qualifications

  • Experience with frontend performance monitoring and optimization.
  • Experience with iOS or Android development, either with the native toolkits or with a framework such as React Native.
  • Experience with designing server side RESTful APIs.
  • Experience with web hosting infrastructure such as CDNs.
