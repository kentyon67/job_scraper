About PayPay

PayPay is a FinTech company that has grown to over 70M (as of July 2025) users since its launch in 2018. Our team is hugely diverse with members from over 50 different countries.

OUR VISION IS UNLIMITED_

We dare to believe that we do not need a clear vision to create a future beyond our imagination. PayPay will always stay true to our roots and realize a vision (future) that no one else can imagine by constantly taking risks and challenging ourselves. With this mindset, you will be presented with new and exciting opportunities on a daily basis and have the opportunity to grow and reach new dimensions that you could never have imagined. We are looking for people who can embrace this challenge, refresh the product at breakneck speed and promote PayPay with professionalism and passion.

Job Description

Main Responsibilities

  • Manage a product management team directly or through other managers 
  • Building a “WoW Experience” for our users by delivering and driving critical products having significant impact on key metrics.
  • Mentoring and educating other product managers of the entire organization
  • Creating platform products that can scale and help build product and services with a long term view
  • Managing and executing decisions about the high-level technical architecture and UI/UX designs of the project. 
  • Write technical PRD’s and work with engineering team and design team and execute the product roadmap
  • Define and prioritize product challenges and opportunities leading to the product growth
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders across functions to understand the user problem and their needs and formulate achievable requirements for development teams.
  • Develop a deep understanding of customer experience, identify and fill product gaps and generate new ideas that grow market share, improve customer experience and drive growth.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in a technology related field or at least 3yrs of technical experience.
  • At Least 4 years of Product Management or an equivalent role
  • Business level of English language (written, verbal)

Preferred Qualifications

  • Masters degree in a technology related field or at least 3yrs of technical experience.
  • Ability to break down any given technical or business problem into deliverables while keeping the stakeholders and business impact in mind. 
  • Track record of working in a very fast paced environment or a startup or working on user facing products
  • Have an extreme bias towards action basically, you need to have the Get Things Done attitude.
  • Possess excellent oral, written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills.
  • Japanese language proficiency is preferred
