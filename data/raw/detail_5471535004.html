Job Description

PayPay is looking for a Cloud Platform Engineer for PayPay's Cloud Based payment system. This role will be responsible for the engineering of Cloud Infrastructure Platforms to provide robust, reliable and cost-efficient Platform so that we can deliver the best experience for our growing customers base.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Architect and build a robust, scalable and stable infrastructure
  • Architect and build Infrastructure that is easily maintainable during patching and upgrades without downtime
  • Architect and build Infrastructure with AWS regional level failure tolerance
  • Work together with our Security Engineers to provision a secure Infrastructure
  • Automations for AWS Deployments to ensure fast delivery of Cloud Services to our developers
  • Provide through Automations means for developers to easily deploy resources on AWS

Tech Stack

  • AWS:
    • VPC, EC2, ECS, EKS, Lambda, Cloudfront, MWAA, RDS, ElastiCache, DynamoDB, Opensearch, S3, CloudWatch, Cognito, SQS, KMS, Secret Manager, KMS, MSK, SES, Transfer Family, ClientVPN, Shield, WAF, GuardDuty, IAM Identity Center, Organization, WorkSpaces, CodeCommit, CodeBuild, CodeDeploy, CodePipeline, CloudFormation and other services
  • Terraform, Github Actions, Prometheus, Grafana, Atlantis, ArgoCD, OpenTelemetry, Wiz, Backstage

Qualifications

  • More than 5 years of technical and architectural experience in AWS-based infrastructure platforms
  • Extensive technical hands-on experience with computing, networking, storage, and analytical services on Latest AWS Services
  • Experience with Terraform based infrastructure as code (IAC) on AWS
  • Experience in production level infrastructure change management and releases for high traffic & business-critical systems
  • Experience in Cloud infrastructure and platform systems availability, performance and cost management
  • Experience as an AWS administrator, including Multi AWS organization management, managing 50+ AWS accounts and 100+ users
  • Strong understanding of cloud security best practices in Fintech Industry
  • Demonstrated skill in programming and scripting languages, including Python, Shell Scripting, Go, and Rust
  • Excellent oral, written, and interpersonal communication skills
  • Proficiency in either English or Japanese

Preferred Qualifications

  • Certified at the AWS Professional or Specialty level
  • Experience in payment industry compliance standards such as PCI DSS 
  • Bachelor’s degree and above in a Computer Science related field
  • Experience with other cloud service providers (e.g GCP, Azure)
  • Experience with Kubernetes (CKA, CKAD or CKS)
  • Experience in AI/ML infrastructure design and operations
  • ​​Experience using and contributing to Open Source tools
  • Published papers / blogs / articles
  • Bilingual in English and Japanese is nice to have, but not required
  • JLPT Level 3 or above
